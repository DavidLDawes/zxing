Welcome to the KochZap App

We help keep the Koch Brothers out of your wallet!

Our simple app has 2 ways to help identify products from Koch Industries or made from one of their brands.

  1. Handy logo reference to identify Koch brands/logos
  2. Scan a product UPC label to check for Koch Industries company codes

Most of the time simply checking the brand/logo reference can steer you away from their products. You can always use the scanning function to verify an individual product is not from a Koch Industries company. You will quickly learn which products to avoid in your lifestyle, and then only need to use the Koch Zap app for an occasional reminder.

  1. Identify Koch Brand Logos - The lower or right-hand portion of the KZ app displays a scrollable image composed of many Koch Industries product and brand logos to avoid.

    Simply refer to this list when shopping and avoid these Koch brand name products and products made using Koch branded materials.

  2. Scan a Product - You can use the KZ app scan function to read a typical product UPC label.

    To initiate a product scan simply press the KochZap button at the top or left of the screen.

    The first time the scanning function is used it will require allowing the use of your camera. Once in the scanning mode you will see a scan widow in which to center the product UPC code.

    You can use your Volume Up/Down buttons to switch the camera light on/off, which can help in low light settings. If the UPC company code for the product scanned is not associated with Koch Industries you will get the Thumbs Up response.

    You will get the Thumbs Down response when the UPC code registers as a Koch Industries related company.

Given the dynamic nature of Koch Industries businesses, the KochZap app can't guarantee accuracy in determining Koch products, though we try tp do our best to keep up with changes. A large percentage of Koch Industries sales are to other companies, providing materials for products sold by those companies, hence a scan will not show a Koch relationship. That is why it is important to become familiar with Koch brand logos, like Dacron and Stainmaster, so as to avoid products made from them.

More information about the KochZap app, Koch Industries, their subsidiaries and facilities, and news related to them can be found at our website - kochzap.com
